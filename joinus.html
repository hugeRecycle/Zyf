Postdoctor

1. Recruitment position:Postdoc (1-3)

2. Job responsibilities:
(1) Independently carry out research on related topics and publish high-level academic papers;
(2) Independently or cooperatively apply for national, provincial or enterprise cooperative research projects;
(3) Assisting and supervising doctoral students and interns;
(4) Participate in the daily management and construction of the laboratory.

3. Job requirements:
(1) Under the age of 35; Have obtained or will obtain a doctorate degree;
(2) Graduates of computer vision, natural language processing, machine learning and other related majors from well-known universities at home and abroad;
(3) Good mathematical foundation, strong programming ability, good English reading and writing skills, familiar with Python language and PyTorch framework;
(4) Have a strong interest in scientific research, work actively, have a rigorous scientific research attitude and good communication skills, team spirit;
(5) Have published at least 3 papers in authoritative international journals or top conferences in the field;
(6) Have certain experience in medical artificial intelligence, and pay attention to its frontier progress is preferred (bonus points, not required).

4. How to apply:
(1) Registration time: long-term recruitment, interested candidates please submit application materials as soon as possible.
(2)Please send your resume to zhengyefeng@westlake.edu.cn with the subject line "Postdoc candidate + name".

Those who meet the requirements and pass the preliminary examination will be notified to arrange an interview. The job Posting is valid until the position is filled.

Research assistant or visiting student

1. Recruitment position:Research assistant or visiting student (5)

2. Job responsibilities:
(1) Assist the scientific research team to carry out the scientific research work of the laboratory;
(2) Those with outstanding ability can be arranged to be independently responsible for the scientific research sub-projects.

3. Job requirements:
(1) Computer vision, natural language processing, machine learning and other related majors;
(2) Research assistants need to work offline at Westlake University;
(3) Good mathematical foundation, strong programming ability, good English reading and writing skills, familiar with Python language and PyTorch framework;
(4) Have a strong interest in scientific research, work actively, have an honest scientific research attitude and good communication skills, team spirit;
(5) Published papers in authoritative international journals or top conferences in the field;
(6) Have certain experience in medical artificial intelligence, and pay attention to its frontier progress is preferred (bonus points, not required).

4. How to apply:
(1) Registration time: long-term recruitment, interested candidates please submit application materials as soon as possible.
(2) Application materials: Please send your resume to zhengyefeng@westlake.edu.cn. Please indicate "Job title - Applicant's name" in the subject line.

For those who meet the requirements and pass the preliminary examination, they will be notified to arrange an interview, and those who do not receive an interview notice within three months can waive the waiting.
